STM32CubeF7 Firmware Package V1.5.0 / 23-September-2016
Main Changes
  • Maintenance release of the STM32Cube_FW_F7 package to:
    • add the support of the STemWin V5.32.
    • add the support of the updated LibJPEG version.
    • fix HAL drivers known defects and enhancements implementation.
    • add the support of DSI-HDMI adapter in STM32F769I_EVAL and STM32F769I-Discovery BSP drivers.
    • update LCD_DSI STM32F769I-Discovery and STM32F769I_EVAL examples to support the HDMI.
