STM32CubeL0 Firmware Package V1.3.0 / 9-September-2015

    • STM32L073xx,STM32L072xx,STM32L071xx and STM32L083xx,STM32L082xx,STM32L081xx.
    • STM32L031xx and STM32L041xx.
